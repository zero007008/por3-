About the Journey

My digital journey began with the fundamental building blocks: JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I started by tackling small frontend projects, focusing on responsive design and user experience. This foundation quickly grew into an ambition to master the entire stack.

I soon expanded into full-stack development, integrating server logic and databases. The next frontier was leveraging emerging technologies—I dedicated time to exploring AI tools, automation scripts, and LLM APIs to streamline processes and create dynamic, smart applications.

Today, PJ Digi World is a convergence of these skills: not just coding, but building cohesive digital presences, from creative branding and bespoke logos (often AI-assisted) to developing viable digital business models and automated systems.
